Cupcake

A cupcake is a small, individual-sized cake that is typically baked in a paper or foil cup. These sweet treats are usually topped with frosting and decorated with colorful sprinkles or other edible decorations. Cupcakes come in a variety of flavors, including chocolate, vanilla, and fruit, and can be customized to suit any taste. They are often served at parties and special occasions or enjoyed as a tasty treat any time of day. Cupcakes are a beloved dessert around the world and have become a popular symbol of celebration and joy.
